The private social network for preschools and parents

Kinderloop allows Preschools to post news, photos and video to parents directly from their mobile device.

Signup for a free trial

Three things we do

  • Record moments in your preschool
    Send photos and news from your preschool straight your parents
  • Engage your parents
    Look out for each other and send updates to keep the neighborhood safe.
  • Share goods and recommendations
    Find a great babysitter or trusty dentist. Borrow a ladder or sell that old bookcase.
  • See a neighborhood in action

Photos and News for Parents - Safe & Secure

  • Capture Moments

    Easily take pictures and reduce the time spent on documenting.

  • Engage Parents

    Develop a positive and constructive relationship with parents.

  • Safe Reporting Time

    Robust reporting that can be produced as required with authentic record keeping.
